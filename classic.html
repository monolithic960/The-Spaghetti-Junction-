Same recipes since 1989.

The Spaghetti Junction opened on Charlestown Road in 1989 with a simple idea: real Italian food, made fast, at a price a family can actually afford.

Over 35 years later, not much has changed — and that's the point. The baked spaghetti is still baked to order. The garlic bread still gets its own fan mail. And you can still feed the whole table without thinking twice about the bill.
